  • Number1

    Bridging Gaps for Young Composers (BGYC) curriculum aims to establish a music teaching method which develops the kind of musician who is able to think of their music practice beyond the limitations of the stage. The curriculum aims to inspire musicians into industries such as engineering, medicine, technology, astronomy etc by revealing the intersections of thought as the fundamental foundation of the BGYC teaching method. We hope to be able to bring this curriculum to more young and aspiring musicians around the world.
