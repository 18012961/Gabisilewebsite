The (BGYC) Creator

Gabi Motuba

Gabi Motuba is a South African award-winning jazz vocalist, composer and music educator. She studied at the Tshwane University of Technology in South Africa where she majored in jazz studies and graduated with a bachelor's degree.

In 2015, Gabi was commissioned to co-compose music for vocals, cello, piano and drums, during a Swiss Artist Residency program facilitated by Prohelvetia. The compositions became part of her debut duo album, Sanctum Sanctorium featuring South African drummer Tumi Mogorosi and Swiss pianist Malcolm Braff. In 2017, Gabi composed 10 string quartet compositions which formed part of her debut solo album, Tefiti-Goddess of Creation.

The album Tefiti was nominated for a 2019 South African Music Award for Best Alternative Album.

In 2019, Gabi was commissioned by the South African Music Rights Organization (SAMRO) to compose and arrange a composition (Those Lost in Time) which has been published on their website. In 2021, Gabi was commissioned by Concerts SA and Kaya FM to produce 5 string quartet compositions, under the project The Sabbath, which were then published as part of the Kaya fm Artist Residency Program. The podcast is available on the Kaya fm website
