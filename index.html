Bridging Gaps for Young Composers (BGYC) curriculum aims to establish a music teaching method which develops the kind of musician who is able to think of their music practice beyond the limitations of the stage. The curriculum aims to inspire musicians into industries such as engineering, medicine, technology, etc by revealing the intersections of thought as the fundamental foundation of the BGYC teaching method.
